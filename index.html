Research Scope

A Walk-Lane Navigation System to Navigate the visually impaired people safely through the Walk-Lanes - Providing a smart solution to Navigate through the Walk-Lanes with the aid of Machine learning and Computer Vision

Road Sign Detection for Visually impaired people - Presenting a real-time system that can recognize a variety of indoor and outdoor navigational indicators placed over clear backgrounds to help visually impaired people.

Pothole and Obstacle Detection - Recognizing obstacles, walking surfaces, and potholes using the wearable IoT device and smart cane to provide appropriate information through audio for safe navigation.

Face Recognition and Emotion Detection of Known Individual - Identification of known individuals in various environments and emotion detection of identified individual to increase social awareness and interaction with the community.

Research Problem

  • Visually impaired people face a lot of difficulties while navigating through the Walk-Lanes.
  • Identifying Traffic signs is very important thing for visually impaired people.
  • Identifying obstacles, pothole and surfaces is also a crucial thing. .
  • Identification of known individuals and emotion detection to bridge the gap of social interaction.Â 

Research Gap

  • Problems with the existing Approaches.
  • No Existing System for the Visually impaired people to navigate through the Walk-Lanes.
  • Introducing an alternative system for APS and identifying traffic signs..
  • Identifying surfaces, potholes and obstacles using a single system.
  • Lack of system to identify known individuals in various environments and inability to relay emotional status of the other person.

Research Objectives

  • Provide a smart solution to navigate through the Walk-Lanes with the aid of Machine learning and Computer Vision.
  • Presenting a real-time system that can recognize a variety of indoor and outdoor navigational indicators placed over clear backgrounds to help visually impaired people.
  • Recognizing obstacles, walking surfaces, and potholes using the wearable IoT device and smart cane to provide appropriate information through audio for safe navigation.
  • Provide a solution where system identifies known individuals of the user through face recognition and relay emotional status of the identifies individual through emotion detection mechanism.

Methodology

The proposed approach will be carried out under 4 components

  • Walk-Lane Navigation System - Demo video collection, Separating video into frames, Perform Canny Edge Detection and Hough Transform to identify the lane lines, Implementation of voice commands
  • Road Sign Detection - Demo video collection, Separating video into frames, Identify traffic signs, Alternative system for APS, Implementation of voice commands.
  • Obstacle Detection -Demo video collection, Separating video into frames, Identify Obstacle, pothole and surfaces, Implementing Smart Cane, Implementation of voice commands.
  • Face Recognition and Emotion Detection - Demo video collection, Separating video into frames, Perform face recognition program to detect known individuals, Implementation of emotion detection of identified individual, Implementation of voice commands.
